Process

How ScribeAble works

From request to confirmed scribe in under 5 minutes.

STEP 01
🧑‍💼
Choose your role

Sign in as a person seeking a scribe, or as a volunteer. Your interface adapts to your needs.

STEP 02
📋
Share your requirements

Tell us your exam date, subject, language, location, and what kind of support you need — by typing or voice.

STEP 03
📍
We match nearby scribes

GPS or pincode matching shows available scribes in your locality who fit your language and subject needs.

STEP 04
🤝
Connect and confirm

Send a request. The scribe accepts or declines. Contact details are shared only after both parties agree.

Built for everyone

Accessibility is the core, not an afterthought

Designed from the ground up for persons with visual, motor, hearing, and cognitive differences.

🔊

Voice Assistant

Fill any form completely by voice. The assistant reads questions aloud and records your spoken answers.

🖐️

Sign Language Mode

Scribes who know Indian Sign Language are flagged. Request ISL-aware support directly in your form.

👁️

Screen Reader Ready

All labels, buttons, and forms are fully compatible with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.

🔤

Adjustable Text Size

Switch between normal, large, and extra-large text from the bar at the top of every page.

🌓

Dark & Light Mode

Toggle between dark and light themes instantly. Both modes meet WCAG AA contrast standards.

⌨️

Full Keyboard Navigation

Every feature reachable by Tab, Enter, and arrow keys — no mouse required.

🌐

Multilingual Interface

Switch to Hindi, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, and more. Scribes are also matched by language preference.

📢

Read-Aloud Scribes

Filter for scribes willing to read exam questions aloud — essential for visual or reading disabilities.
